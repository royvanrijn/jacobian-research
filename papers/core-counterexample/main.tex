\begin{abstract}
We study an explicit polynomial map of affine three-space with constant
nonzero Jacobian and three distinct rational points in one fiber.  We give
exact algebraic certificates, identify the source with a marked-simple-root
incidence for binary cubics, determine the image and fiber structure over
the complex numbers, and extend the construction to a normalized weighted
marked-root family with symmetric monodromy.
\end{abstract}
