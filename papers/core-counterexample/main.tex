\begin{abstract}
We construct a noninjective etale endomorphism of affine three-space from a
marked-divisor cover of $\mathbb P^1$.  An auxiliary conic and residual
intersection identify its source with affine three-space without coordinates.
We then give an explicit polynomial realization, exact algebraic
certificates, the image and fiber structure over the complex numbers, and a
normalized weighted marked-root family with symmetric monodromy.
\end{abstract}
