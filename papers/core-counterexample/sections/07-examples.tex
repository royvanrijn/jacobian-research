\section{Examples}

The original map is the degree-three marked-root model and has monodromy
$S_3$. Its cubic discriminant gives the complete $3/1/0$ fiber table.

For a degree-$n$ admissible weighted seed, Theorems~\ref{thm:weighted} and
\ref{thm:monodromy} give generic degree $n$ and monodromy $S_n$.  Canonical
seeds $H_d(W)=W^d(1-W)$ and small deformations provide exact test cases for
the normalized boundary charts.  These examples are included to illustrate
the theorem, not to replace its all-degree proof.

The repository verification suite checks the cubic charts, the structural
weighted determinant, reconstruction identities, discriminant normalization,
and representative seeds in exact arithmetic.
