\section{Exact verification}

On the dense open set $x\ne0$, introduce
\[
t=y+1/x,\qquad r=2/x,\qquad c=F_3(x,y,z),
\]
and write $(a,b,c)=F(x,y,z)$. Direct substitution yields
\[
a=t^2+\frac{rt}{2}-ct^3,
\qquad
b=r+4t-3ct^2.
\]
The two Jacobian factors are
\[
\det\frac{\partial(a,b,c)}{\partial(t,r,c)}=\frac r2,
\qquad
\det\frac{\partial(t,r,c)}{\partial(x,y,z)}=-2x.
\]
Since $r=2/x$, their product is $-2$.  Both $\det DF$ and $-2$ are
polynomials, so equality on the dense open proves equality everywhere.
Substitution of the three displayed source points proves the collision.

The repository contains two exact implementations and a separately authored,
pinned Lean 4 formalization.  The precise commands and scope are listed in
the accompanying verification directory; none of the geometric theorems is
needed for this certificate.
