# Claim ledger

This ledger records the evidentiary status of each major theorem stated in
the README.  “Executable certificate” means an exact computation of the
finite identity or model named in that cell.  It does not silently promote a
bounded computation into a proof of an all-degree quantifier.  “Regression
only” lists computations whose role is testing a written uniform proof.

For the directed proof graph, exact quantifiers, internal lemma dependencies,
and claim-by-claim unresolved obligations, see
[Claim dependency graph and proof ledger](notes/CLAIM_DEPENDENCY_GRAPH.md).
The proof chain through C04 is also collected without later dependencies in
[Foundational geometry](notes/FOUNDATIONAL_GEOMETRY.md).

The verification target names refer to the targets in the repository
`Makefile`.  An unchecked independent audit means that the repository has not
yet recorded a clean-room proof or execution independent of the present
arguments and implementations.

## Known gaps exposed by the ledger

- The scripts accompanying C04--C11 verify identities and bounded examples;
  they are not formal proofs of the all-degree quantifiers.
- C13 now has an explicit bad-prime certificate, a complete twist-hypothesis
  audit, exact boundary terms, and a coarse effective error; an independent
  arithmetic-geometry audit remains pending.
- C15's external hypotheses have been audited against the primary statements,
  and a standard-library exact implementation independently regenerates both
  forms.  Clean-environment archival reproduction remains desirable.
- No all-degree family theorem has a formal proof-assistant artifact.
- C16 now gives the canonical normalized graph blow-up and the explicit
  minimal transverse toric sequences.  An independent birational-geometry
  audit remains pending; a noncanonical global threefold log resolution is
  deliberately not claimed.
- C17--C20 and the `k=5,6` calculations are exact local regressions; C18 now
  proves the general global equalizer by the universal Wronskian.
- C22 now has two unrelated uniform proofs: invariant descent and the
  divided-power conductor-ribbon construction. Independent exact Gröbner
  regressions extend through `k=6`, and the dependency-free deformation audit
  checks its structural identities through `k=8`.
- C21 is now the global affine-rigidity closed-immersion theorem. Its remaining
  obligation is an independent audit of the completed Boolean descent, not an
  unresolved collision case.
- C24 proves the finite normalized jet recurrence but not the arithmetic and
  coordinate-equivalence classification of all polynomial cancellation
  branches. Uniform irreducibility of its truncated-binomial parameter
  polynomial is deliberately not claimed.
- Several exact scripts under `scripts/` are exploratory or ancillary and are
  intentionally outside `make verify`; their absence is not evidence for a
  theorem unless a ledger row names them.
