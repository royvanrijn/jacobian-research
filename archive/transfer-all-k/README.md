# Archived all-k transfer programme

This directory preserves the C18, C21, and C22 programme-level arguments,
the transfer-dependent portion of C23, and superseded all-`k`
interpretations of C17--C20.  Nothing was deleted.

The archive was created after the proposed transverse associated graded

\[
\mathbb Q[s,a]/(\operatorname{coeff}\operatorname{rem}_S(A^2))
\]

was refuted at `k=2`: the true transfer tangent cone also contains `X^3`,
while the proposed quadratic algebra specializes to
`Q[X,Y]/(XY,Y^2)` and is positive-dimensional.  See the active
[counterexample](../../experimental/transfer-and-cancellation/QUADRATIC_REMAINDER_ALGEBRA.md).

Files here are retained for provenance only.  They are not part of current
proof navigation and may contain claims explicitly superseded by the
counterexample.  The scripts subdirectory likewise contains historical
all-`k`, ribbon, allocation, and quotient regressions.

The independent C23 affine-complement results remain active in
[UNIVERSAL_FACTORIZATION_GEOMETRY.md](../../experimental/transfer-and-cancellation/UNIVERSAL_FACTORIZATION_GEOMETRY.md).
The exact `k=2,3,4` local transfer algebras remain active only as bounded
examples in
[TRANSFER_BLOCKS.md](../../experimental/transfer-and-cancellation/TRANSFER_BLOCKS.md).
