# Historical dependency notes

The repository formerly maintained a numbered claim graph alongside several
separate status documents.  That graph became stale as proofs were merged,
the transfer programme was refuted and archived, and the cancellation family
was split into construction, arithmetic, boundary, and rigidity results.

It is intentionally not preserved as a second status ledger.  The only
current evidence table is [`STATUS.md`](../../STATUS.md).

The active mathematical dependency chain is now described by names:

1. the foundational Keller map and rational collision;
2. the cubic marked-root model;
3. the exact cubic image and nonproperness theorem;
4. the weighted marked-root theorem;
5. the extended discriminant, contact, image, and compactification results;
6. the cancellation construction and its arithmetic and boundary geometry;
7. the marked-root degreewise stable-multiplicity theorem.

The abandoned transfer route is documented separately in
[`archive/transfer-program/`](../transfer-program/).  Its failed all-parameter
filtrations are historical evidence only and are not dependencies of an active
result.
