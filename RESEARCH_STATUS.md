## C24 next milestone

**Prove a coordinate-independent invariant showing when two
resolvent-cancellation constructions cannot be polynomially left--right
equivalent.**

The current finite-normalization signature separates C24 with `r>=2` from
generic weighted seeds. The unresolved test case is `r=1`, `m>1`, where the
known degree, monodromy, divisorial ramification, residue degrees, and generic
sheet-loss data agree. The next work should compare conductor ideals,
boundary-intersection local rings, or filtered valuation semigroups. No other
claim should be extended while this milestone is active.

## C22 corrective milestone

The divided-power ribbon norm misses a second-order collision class already
for `k=2`. The next valid milestone is to prove a monic straightening basis
of size `2^k` directly in the completed square/cube factorization ring, or to
find a value of `k` where rank or flatness fails. See the
[counteraudit](experimental/transfer-and-cancellation/C22_CONDUCTOR_RIBBON_AUDIT.md).
